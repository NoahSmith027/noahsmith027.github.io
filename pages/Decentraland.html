Changeling is a virtual reality narrative mystery game that I worked on for a co-op over Fall 2020 as a Developer. In Changeling, you play as Aurelia, a dream-walker whose gift is the ability to see through the eyes of anyone she touches. You are tasked with helping this family figure out what is wrong with their child, and as you contact each member, you see through the lens of their hopes and fears of what the child is.

The project is made using Unreal Engine 4 and tested using Oculus Rift and Quest virtual reality headsets as well as mouse and keyboard. My contributions have been focused on gameplay mechanic programming, user interface development, and bug fixing, but I have also worked on optimization, level design, and audio design.
