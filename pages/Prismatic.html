Prismatic is a light puzzle game that was made in a group of five over a period of five weeks. It was made to be like the laser-based mobile games, but this game had 360 degree rotation and 2.5D graphics instead of 2D. Work was split up mostly into each person working on one piece and one artist that made the models. I worked on the "prisms", or the goals to beat the level. I also contributed three levels (not all shown in the video), and set up the UI.
