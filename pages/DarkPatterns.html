Dark Patterns is a group project (The team being called "We're Getting Off Track a Bit" and including me, Jonathan So, Vince DiCocco, and Charley Link) that is being designed to be entered for a co-op opportunity throught MAGIC Studios on RIT campus. The above screenshot is from the first iteration of a project that is currently being overhauled. The inital premise of the game was an entertaining education game that teaches about dark patterns. The definition of dark patterns is: Dark Patterns are tricks used in websites and apps that make you buy or sign up for things that you didn't mean to. This game was being made to teach people of varying ages about these patterns and what to do about them / with them. The game is being changed to be more entertaining as the first iteration of the project was too focused on being educational.

My role in Dark Patterns' first iteration was some visual design sections for a mock website. So far towards the newer iteration I have had a large role in the new overall design and narrative.
