This project is an interactive storytelling experience that puts the player in the shoes of an explorer in the medieval times searching for a rumored fortune of treasure despite the risks and rumors that come with it. The project heavily focuses on different focuses of narrative, namely embedded, enacted, and evoked narratives, and on player interactivity in storytelling.

The project was made in Unreal Engine 4 using multiple custom-made blueprint systems and multiple resource packs for models and textures.
